I'm

Designer, Illustrator, Painter, Animator

I am a platform agnostic Visual Designer based out of Vancouver BC. Trained in Graphic Design, Animation Film Making, and Traditional Painting, I love creating stories through all mediums possible.

My design philosophy is based on effective storytelling. I love to use multiple mediums to express and deliver assignments. Exposure to numerous cultures and various mediums of artistic expression like painting, pottery, animation, and cinema has had a profound influence in building my foundation more as a Designer than an Artist.
